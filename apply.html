Become a Life Progress Coach

Join the founding pilot cohort of student coaches. Support others, deepen your sense of purpose, and help shape a new developmental peer coaching model at Global College.

What is Life Progress Coaching?

Life Progress Coaches walk alongside peers to listen deeply, ask thoughtful questions, and support meaningful forward movement under real-life constraints.

This is not psychotherapy or counseling. It is a developmental, non-clinical coaching approach focused on meaning, direction, agency, and supportive growth.

What You Will Do

Summer Semester: Training & Development

  • Complete the application and a 20-minute interview with the Lead Coach.
  • Participate in a 2-day intensive workshop.
  • Learn the LPT framework: Recovery, Renewal, and the 4T model.
  • Engage in real coaching conversations across 5 cases.
  • Review training materials and provide pilot feedback.

Autumn Semester: Practice & Implementation

  • Continue supervised practice with additional individual clients.
  • Participate in group co-facilitation.
  • Attend LPT Labs and guided practice sessions.
  • Attend supervision meetings with the Lead Coach.
  • Submit session records within 24 hours after each session.
  • Contribute to ongoing evaluation and system refinement.

Pilot Training & Practice Pathway

This pilot pathway outlines the training, practice, supervision, and reflection activities planned for the Summer and Autumn semesters. Participation in this phase provides foundational preparation for future certification, subject to formal institutional approval.

  1. Application & Interview: Complete the application and a 20-minute interview with the Lead Coach.
  2. Intensive Training: Participate in structured training introducing the LPC approach, coaching boundaries, and core coaching skills.
  3. Practice Development: Engage in real coaching conversations with 5 cases and document the learning process.
  4. Supervised Practice: Continue coaching practice with additional individual clients, group co-facilitation, and ongoing supervision.
  5. LPT Labs: Participate in structured practice sessions to develop coaching skills, structural awareness, and peer learning.
  6. Competency Assessment: Develop and demonstrate relational, reflective, ethical, and practical coaching capacity.
  7. Reflective Portfolio: Submit a short reflective portfolio including learning insights, anonymized experience, and emerging coaching philosophy.

Certification Path & Institutional Status

Please note that the Life Progress Center (LPC) is currently in its Pilot & Development Phase. Official Coach Certification is subject to the formal institutional approval of the Center by the College.

Eligibility: Students who successfully complete the Summer and Autumn training cycles and pass the competency assessment will be pre-qualified for certification.

Issuance: Official certificates will be formally issued upon the successful establishment and administrative approval of the Center.

Who Should Apply?

We are looking for students who are:

  • Interested in coaching, education, leadership, communication, or personal growth.
  • Strong listeners who are open-minded and reflective.
  • Responsible, reliable, and willing to receive feedback.
  • Able to commit across both Summer and Autumn semesters.
  • Open to learning developmental coaching skills in a non-clinical peer support model.

You do not need to:

  • Have all the answers.
  • Have prior coaching experience.
  • Act as a counselor or therapist.
  • Fix other people’s problems.
