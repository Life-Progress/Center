Our Stance

我們不以誇大的保證作為承諾，而是專注於可持續的成長與真實的進展。

我們相信生命的發展是方向性的，而非保證的結果；

逆境與不確定不是要被消除的暫時障礙，而是成長結構的一部分。

我們的角色是陪伴你在現實的約束中，持續校準、恢復、更新，
一步一步朝向更成熟、更具意義的生命歷程。

我們提供教練式支持與同儕支撐，不提供心理治療或精神醫療；若有嚴重困擾，建議轉介至合格專業。

Rather than offering exaggerated guarantees, we focus on sustainable growth and real progress.

We believe that development is directional, not a guaranteed outcome;

Adversity and uncertainty are not temporary obstacles to be eliminated, but integral parts of the structure of growth.

Our role is to accompany you within the constraints of reality, continuously calibrating, recovering, and renewing, step by step toward a more mature and meaningful life journey.

We provide coaching and peer support—not psychotherapy or psychiatric care. For severe distress, we recommend referral to licensed professionals.

The LP Method

一個簡單流程：先定位，再前進（不靠硬撐）。

A simple flow: locate first, then move—without brute forcing.

STEP 1

2R 定位（Recovery 恢復 · Renewal 更新）

你现在更需要「恢复」（减轻压力/稳住）还是「更新」（重建方向/活出价值）？两者可并行，但先看张力。

Do you need more Recovery (relieve stress / stabilize) or Renewal (rebuild direction / live your values) right now? They can co-exist—start by noticing the tension.

STEP 2

4T 卡点（Transparency 通透 · Transformation 转化 · Transition 转向 · Togetherness 同行）

你卡在哪里：看不清（通透）、鬆動不了（转化）、做不到（转向）、没支撑（同行）？

Where are you stuck: can’t see clearly (Transparency), can’t shift internally (Transformation), can’t carry it out (Transition), or lack support (Togetherness)?

STEP 3

微支撑前进

不追求「立刻变好」。我们设计一个可做到的小步子，并用关系与环境把它撑住。

We don’t aim for “getting better immediately.” We design one doable next step—and let relationships and environment hold it in place.

BOUNDARY / 界线

学生教练提供同侪「微支撑」支持；若涉及严重焦虑/抑郁、创伤、失眠失控、自伤意念等，我们会优先建议转介到校方或合格心理专业。

Student coaches provide peer-based micro-support. If concerns involve severe anxiety or depression, trauma, uncontrolled insomnia, or self-harm ideation, we prioritize referral to university services or licensed mental health professionals.
